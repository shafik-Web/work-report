01 — Introduction

What is NeoClick?

NeoClick is an AI-powered photobooth application that transforms ordinary photos into stunning artwork using advanced Google Gemini AI technology.

Perfect for events, corporate gatherings, exhibitions, and marketing activations. Create memorable photo experiences that your guests will love and share.

🎯 Key Highlights
  • ✓ Real-time AI image transformation
  • ✓ 12+ unique AI art styles
  • ✓ Custom brand frame overlay
  • ✓ QR code for instant download
  • ✓ Email photo directly to guests
  • ✓ Admin dashboard & analytics
